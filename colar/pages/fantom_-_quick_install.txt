======Fantom - Quick Install======
  - Download fantom from [[http://www.fantom.org/]]

  - Extract the fantom archive
<code | example>
unzip fantom-1.0.57.zip
</code>
  - Fix permissions (unixes)
<code>
chmod +x fantom-1.0.57/adm/unixsetup
./fantom-1.0.57/adm/unixsetup
</code>
  - Add Fantom binaries to your path
<code>
export PATH=$PATH:/home/me/fantom-1.0.57/bin
</code>
  - Test Fantom
run ''fan'' should say "Fantom launcher" ....
