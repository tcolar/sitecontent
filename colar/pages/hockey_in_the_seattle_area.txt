====== Hockey in the Greater Seattle Area / Stick n Puck======
**Last Updated 8/19/15**

===== Major adult Ice Hockey leagues in the Seattle area=====
The major adult leagues are:

   * [[http://www.gshockey.com | the Greater Seattle Hockey League]] (Everett, Mtlake Terrace, Lynwood, Kent, Kent ShoWare)

  * [[http://snokinghockeyleague.com/ | Snoking Hockey league]] (Renton, Kingsgate) - New 2015

  * [[http://www.psaha.pointstreaksites.com/view/psaha/rainier-hockey-league | The Rainier hockey league]] (N. Tacoma)

  * [[http://www.cascadehockey.com/ | The Cascade Hockey League ]] (Lynnwood, Everett)

  * [[http://www.olyview.com/ | Olympic View Hockey League]] (Mtlake Terrace)
