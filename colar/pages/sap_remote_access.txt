====== Sap Remote Access ======

Open a connection for SAP service

For connection to the remote access machine (windows terminal server) on Cedar.

Login into http://service.sap.com/ \\ 
Click on "Remote connections" \\
Click on "Maintain Connections" \\
Click on TPT \\
Click "Maintain Connections" \\
And open a "R/3 support" and a "Windows Terminal" connections for 5 days. \\

Then SAP can do this: \\

From the SAP connection platform, connect To \\
Pacific Coast Feather / BW / TPT \\
Then use the "Cedar" connection to connect to the machine (Windows terminal server).
