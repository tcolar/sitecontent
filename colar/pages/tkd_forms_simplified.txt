======TKD Forms Simplified======
My "Simplified" and annotated version of this:
[[TKD_forms]]

Syntax:
N,E,S,W: directions. L:left R: right. Dbl: Double. DP: double punch. CW: clockwise CC: counter clock
Example "1:  W(L) L Low Block, DP(R,L)" means Facing West(Left foot in front), Left arm low block, punch right hand, punch left hand
===White Belt===
JoonBi(ready), Kibon Poomse(fundamental forms), JahJeh(self control) or Chigi(hand attack)?? JoonBi... turn 90 cc
1:  W(L) L Low Block, DP(R,L) turn 180 cw
2:  E(R) R Low Block, DP(L,R) turn 180 cc
3:  W(L) L Middle Block, L jab, R Punch  turn 180 cw
4:  E(R) R Middle block, R Jab, L punch : turn 90 cc 
5:  N(L) L High Block, DP(R,L) Step Forward
6:  N(R) R High Block, DP(L,R) Stay
7:  N(R) R/L Dbl Middle block (open hand across face), DP(R,L) Step back
8:  N(L) L/R Dbl Middle block (open hand across face), DP(L,R) Stay
9:  N(R) L Low Block/Back-fist, DP(R,L) Step Forward
10: N(L) R Low Block/Back-fist, DP(L,R) Stay
11: N(L) R Knife + block THEN R cross Knife + block Step Forward (No DP)
12: N(R) L Knife + block THEN L cross Knife + block (No DP) turn 90 cc
13: W(L) L Dbl Windshield blocks, DP(L,R) turn 180 cw
14: E(R) R Dbl Windshield blocks, DP(R,L) turn 90cc
15: N(L) L/R Dbl Inside blocks (to the side), DP(L,R)
16: N-S-S(L) L 180 with L knife-hand THEN point L heel South, R 360 R knife-hand. Stay
17: S(R) DP(R,L), then Dbl uppercuts(R,L)
18: N(L) L 180 DP(L,R), Dbl uppercuts(L,R)
Kuman(end fight) ... 

**Notes:**

  * 5-12: all north: Fwd, Stay, Back, Stay, Fwd, Stay, Fwd.
Stay: after high blocks, After back-fists
Back: Between Open Hand blocks.
  * 11: (Order ?) Right hand facing right ear (elbow out), neck strike. Then right hand facing left hear, neck strike.
  * 12: (Order ?) Right hand facing right ear (elbow out), neck strike. Then right hand facing left hear, neck strike.
  * 16: Start with left facing right ear, palm facing sky. Spin 180 west and complete neck strike South.
Then: Start with right hand facing left ear, pivot left heel toward south, Spit 360 east and complete neck strike South.
